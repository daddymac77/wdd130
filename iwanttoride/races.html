Local club race

Local Club Race is great way to test your skills and limits. One of the advantages of local club races is you dont have to travel far to participate in a race. It is ussualy held near your city, or if your lucky, right there in your city! You can compete with familiar faces. Race with your friends. Winner takes all type of race

Winner takes all is type of race where the winner will take home the price, which is the registration fee to participates in the race. There are no categories. It's one against all and all against one. Rules are simple, and participants will set the rules sometimes. If you're up to it, ronda de orchard here in Tagum city always have a winners takes all races once or twice a month
